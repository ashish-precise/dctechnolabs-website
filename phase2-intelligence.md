## 3. What Customers Actually Complain About (from competitor reviews)

These are the real fears every Ahmedabad client has when hiring a web agency. Your entire site should directly address each one.

### The 5 Core Fears:

**1. "I'll pay upfront and they'll disappear"**
- Source: Multiple DNG reviews mention advance payment + no work delivered
- How DCT addresses this: Milestone-based payment policy, written agreement, project timeline upfront

**2. "The reviews are fake"**
- Source: Reviews explicitly call out fake Google reviews
- How DCT addresses this: Encourage clients to post honest reviews; respond to all reviews publicly

**3. "They'll be great before payment, ghost me after"**
- Source: Multiple agencies praised pre-sale then stopped responding
- How DCT addresses this: Dedicated account manager, defined response time SLA

**4. "I'll get a cheap template dressed up as custom work"**
- Source: One reviewer paid 600 euros for a 50-euro template website
- How DCT addresses this: Show process (discovery, wireframe, design, development), show custom work in portfolio

**5. "They don't understand my actual business goals"**
- Source: Reviewer described an agency that jumped straight to "how many pages?" without asking about objectives
- How DCT addresses this: Discovery call process, requirements document, goal-aligned proposals

Position in the ₹25,000 to ₹1,50,000 range for websites. This places you:

No competitor has neighborhood-specific content. Your existing Ahmedabad page mentions these areas but dedicated sub-pages will rank for hyper-local searches within weeks.

Publish a detailed, honest pricing guide. Competitors all use bait pricing. Being the one agency that says "here's exactly what things cost and why" builds immediate trust and captures the keyword cluster.

People who get burned by DNG search for alternatives. A blog post titled "How to Choose a Trustworthy Web Design Company in Ahmedabad" (covering red flags like fake reviews, advance payment requests, no written contracts) will capture this traffic without naming any competitor.
